Welcome to CS Resources!

The purpose of this website is to provide CS students a place where they can find useful sources of information and study material without spending hours searching the web.

If you find a useful resource somewhere else that is not included in this website, please feel free to submit a request to have it added so that others may also find it!

- ~ λ ~ -

Dozens of books, videos, and lectures on a variety of subjects have been compiled and categorized for your convenience. Each books has their corresponding Amazon link while each video or lecture has a YouTube link.

Subjects include: Data Structures, Algorithms, Compiler Design, Computer Architecture, Discrete Mathematics, Software Engineering, Network Administration, Network Security, Operating Systems, and many others.

- ~ λ ~ -

This website also serves as a general outline of the field of computer science by providing study material related to each of the major branches of computer science.
