Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy a book?

Clicking the book cover image will take you to the Amazon page for that specific book.

Can I submit a book or video to be added to the website?

Yes, you can. Click the “Submit Request” link in the navigation bar and fill out the form in order for us to consider adding new content to the website.

Do you get paid to show these books and videos on your website?

No, the content on this website is picked at the author’s discretion. This website isn’t a form of advertising for other authors or teachers. All images and videos are under a Creative Commons license.
